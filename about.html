On weekdays I'm just your average Joe working 9 to 5 clearing files off the desk and checking items off the To-Do list, on the weekends I pick up my camera and set out to capture the world in all it's glory.

I was born and raised in New Delhi, the city which has everything to offer that a photographer could wish for. Being the elder son I had to step up and take responsibilities of my family after my father passed away but I could never leave my passion aside.

Wanting to pursue my passion seriously and not let it just be a passing fancy, I've enrolled in the Photgraphy specialization course created by the Michigan State University.
